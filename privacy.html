1. Who We Are

Safe2Park? ("the App") is an iOS application developed and operated by Abdu-Mansur Hafiz, an independent developer based in the United States.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at itssafe2park@gmail.com or visit safe2park.org.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account credentials: Your email address and password when you create an account. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are handled by Firebase Authentication.
  • Display name: An optional name you provide during sign-up.
  • Parking sign photos: Photos you voluntarily submit to report parking signs. These are uploaded to Firebase Storage and reviewed by our team before being made public.
  • Problem reports: Text and optional photos submitted via "Report a Problem" in the app.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Location (GPS): Your device's location is used while the app is in use to show nearby parking zones, meters, and garages. We do not track your location in the background or when the app is closed.
  • Parking sessions: When you start a parking timer, we store the session start time, duration, and location so you can view your parking history.
  • App usage data: Basic crash reports and performance data may be collected through Firebase to help us identify and fix technical issues.

2.3 Information stored only on your device

  • Saved (favorite) locations: Locations you star are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. They are not uploaded to our servers.
  • App preferences: Settings like dark mode and accessibility options are stored locally.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only to operate and improve the App:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display real-time parking zone status near your location
  • To send local notifications for parking timer alerts and upcoming restrictions
  • To store and display your parking session history
  • To review and approve user-submitted parking sign photos
  • To respond to problem reports and support requests
  • To identify bugs and improve app performance

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing.

4. Third-Party Services

Safe2Park uses the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Used for user authentication, cloud database (Firestore), and file storage. Firebase may collect usage and crash data subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apple MapKit — Used to render the in-app map and display parking locations. MapKit is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Parking regulation data displayed in the app is publicly available city data and does not involve the collection of personal information.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your account data and parking sessions are stored in Google Firebase, which is hosted on Google Cloud servers in the United States. Firebase applies industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, but no system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at itssafe2park@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information from our servers within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Parking session history is retained for 12 months and then automatically deleted.

To request account deletion, email us at itssafe2park@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of notifications: You can disable push notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings → Notifications → Safe2Park.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at itssafe2park@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Safe2Park is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at itssafe2park@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
